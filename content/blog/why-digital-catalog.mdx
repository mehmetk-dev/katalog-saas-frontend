## Introduction

In today's fast-paced digital world, customers want instant access to information. Traditional print catalogs have become costly and difficult to update. So why is a **digital product catalog** a must for your business?

### 1. Low Cost and Sustainability

Paper, printing, and shipping costs are increasing every day. Once a digital catalog is created, it can be delivered to an unlimited number of people at zero cost. You also strengthen your brand's eco-friendly image by preventing paper waste.

### 2. Instant Updatability

Did a new product arrive? Did the price change? It is impossible to fix this in a print catalog. In a digital catalog, you can make changes in seconds and ensure that all your customers see the most up-to-date information.

### 3. Interactive Experience

Digital catalogs don't just consist of images. By adding videos, animations, and **order via WhatsApp** buttons, you can simplify your customer's buying journey.

### 4. Measurable Analytics

Which product was viewed more? Which page in your catalog was clicked the most? This data is vital for determining your strategy. Platforms like FogCatalog provide you with these analytics automatically.

### 5. Access from Anywhere (The Power of QR Codes)

With a **QR code** on your storefront or business card, your customers can access all your products in seconds from their phones.
